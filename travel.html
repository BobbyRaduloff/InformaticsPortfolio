Travel to Hong Kong

Why visit?

Hong Kong is a beautiful mix of Chinese and Western European culture. Visiting will allow you to experience the best of both cultures.

What to see?

  • Kowloon Walled City Park
  • Temple Street Night Market
  • Man Mo Temple
  • Golden Bauhinia Square

Coolest people from Hong Kong

  1. Jackie Chan
  2. Denise Ho
  3. Tony Leung

Protests

Hong Kong is currently experiencing a wave of political protests. This should not be a problem as long as you stay away from the most crowded urban centers and follow the orders of police.
